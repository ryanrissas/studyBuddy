Insight

I am the first of my family to go to college and for that I am grateful and humbled. I was always told that making it out of poverty was the hardest up hill battle I would face. I am grateful for where I come from and my past because it drives me to become better each and everyday - why you ask? Seeing what my family had to go through for some could never understand and that makes me different. My work ethic is out of this world, I will find a way to get anything done and I will relentlessly not let anything get in my way.
